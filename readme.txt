Allow accessing your WordPress with Mastodon clients. Just enter your own blog URL as your instance.

This plugin works best in combination with the <a href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/friends/">Friends</a> and <a href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/activitypub/">ActivityPub</a> plugins which connects your WordPress blog with the Fediverse through which you can follow people and be followed via the ActivityPub protiocol which is what Mastodon uses.

In combination with this plugin you can then enjoy the plurality of Mastodon clients to access the Fediverse through your blog.
